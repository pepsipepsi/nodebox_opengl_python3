getting a generator representing points along a BezierPath

BezierPath.points(int how many points you want)

leaf = BezierPath()
leaf.moveto(0, 0)
leaf.curveto(50, 50, 0, 150, 0, 200)
leaf.curveto(0, 150, -50, 50, 0, 0)

for i in leaf.points(5):
	print("a point : ", i)
